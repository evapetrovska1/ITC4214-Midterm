The DreamWorks Experience

Our Legacy

Founded in 1994 by Steven Spielberg, Jeffrey Katzenberg, and David Geffen, DreamWorks Animation revolutionized the industry with groundbreaking films like Shrek. We've produced over 30 feature films, won multiple Academy Awards, and became the first studio to truly challenge Disney's animation dominance with our unique storytelling style and technological innovation.

Characters

From Shrek's grumpy charm that subverted fairy tale tropes to Po's unlikely hero journey blending martial arts with Chinese philosophy. Our characters like Hiccup and Toothless from How to Train Your Dragon, the hilarious Madagascar crew, and the colorful Trolls have become childhood companions for millions worldwide, creating franchises that span films, series, and merchandise.

Animation Innovation

We pioneered realistic fur and hair rendering technology, developed proprietary animation software, and consistently push the boundaries of 3D animation. From the stunning water effects in How to Train Your Dragon to the expressive character animations in The Bad Guys, our technical innovations have raised industry standards while serving our creative vision.
